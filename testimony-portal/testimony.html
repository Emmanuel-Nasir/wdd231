Before You Post Your Comments

Comments on Testimonies

  • Don’t post testimonies in comments. Please submit your testimony as a post so that it will appear on a separate page on which users may respond.
  • Leave comments that are in good taste and do not offend the audience or endorse any kind of philosophy involving hate, racism, bigotry, misogyny, etc.
  • We prefer that you not leave email addresses or personal names or information in the body of your comments.
  • Don’t leave promotional links to other sites in comments.
  • testimonyportal.com is not intended for publishing rants or gossip or conspiracy theories. Make sure your comments edify.
  • Do your best to use correct English grammar and spelling. Don’t use all caps when commenting.
  • Please do not make factually inaccurate statements about world history, religion, science, etc. We don’t publish comments that contain information that we know to be false. If you don’t know whether it’s true, don’t state it as true.
